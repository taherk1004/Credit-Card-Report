# Credit Card Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze credit card transactions and revenue generation across different customer segments.

The dashboard provides insights into customer demographics, spending patterns, transaction behavior, and revenue contribution. The objective is to help businesses identify high-value customer groups and make data-driven decisions.

## Key Insights

- Businessmen generated the highest revenue among all occupations.
- Customers aged 40–50 contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Male customers generated higher overall revenue than female customers.
- The Blue card category accounted for the majority of transactions.
- Revenue peaked during Q4 compared to other quarters.
